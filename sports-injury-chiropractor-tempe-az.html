DNS Therapy & Movement Rehabilitation

Dynamic Neuromuscular Stabilization (DNS) therapy focuses on improving posture, breathing patterns, movement coordination, and core stability. Many athletes and active individuals benefit from DNS therapy as part of rehabilitation-focused chiropractic treatment designed to improve muscular balance and natural movement control.

Spinal Decompression & Recovery Support

Spinal decompression therapy gently stretches the spine to reduce pressure on discs and surrounding nerves. This therapy may help support recovery for patients experiencing lower back discomfort, spinal tension, sciatica symptoms, or reduced mobility related to physical activity and repetitive stress.
