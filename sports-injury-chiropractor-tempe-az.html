Sports Injury Chiropractor in Tempe AZ

Sports injuries often create muscle tension, spinal stress, reduced flexibility, and movement limitations that affect athletic performance and overall wellness. Chiropractic care focuses on restoring movement, improving spinal alignment, and helping the body recover naturally.

At Weisbrod Chiropractic, treatment plans are personalized for each patient and may include chiropractic adjustments, rehabilitation exercises, DNS therapy, posture correction, and spinal decompression therapy.

  • Natural pain relief
  • Sports rehabilitation
  • Muscle recovery support
  • Movement rehabilitation
  • Posture correction
  • Spinal stability improvement

DNS Therapy for Athletes

Dynamic Neuromuscular Stabilization (DNS) therapy helps improve posture, breathing patterns, movement control, and core stability. Many athletes benefit from DNS therapy as part of rehabilitation-focused chiropractic care.

Spinal Decompression Therapy

Spinal decompression therapy gently stretches the spine to reduce pressure on discs and surrounding nerves. This treatment may help patients experiencing lower back pain, sciatica symptoms, or spinal tension related to physical activity.
